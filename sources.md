## Extracted Pattern Notes
- Frequent stems: FIRST action, NEXT step, MOST appropriate action, and SUSPECT prompts.
- Strong distractors: right action/wrong time, delayed care, ABC omission, and scope mismatch.
- Rationales emphasize sequence, reassessment, and trend interpretation over rote recall.
